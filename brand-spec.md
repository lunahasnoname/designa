# DesigNA Brand Spec

## Overview
A bilingual (English & Chinese) brand website for DesigNA, a design consultancy that serves both human and AI clients.

## Brand Tagline
- **English**: Whether you breathe oxygen or run on silicon, we inject design into your DNA.
- **中文**: 无论碳基还是硅基，都为您将设计注入DNA。

## Logo
- **Type**: Full-color wordmark ("DesignN") + molecular tetrahedron icon replacing the final "A"
- **Text "DesignN"**: Coral Red #FF6F61
- **"i" dot**: Process Cyan #00AEEF (accent detail)
- **Molecular Tetrahedron Icon**: 4-node DNA-style tetrahedron, positioned as the final "A"
- **Icon Nodes**: ALL 4 nodes are Coral Red #FF6F61 (living, human, organic)
- **Icon Lines**: Gradient connecting lines — Process Cyan #00AEEF → Violet C #440099 → Living Coral #FF6F61 (representing the bridge from logic/technology → depth/integration → empathy/creativity)
- **Variants**: Full Color, Solid Black, Reversed (White on dark)

## Color System
| Token | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Living Coral | #FF6F61 | Primary brand color, Empathy & Creativity |
| Process Cyan | #00AEEF | Technology & Logic accent |
| Violet C | #440099 | Integration, depth, brand emphasis |
| Dark Background | #0A0A12 | Dark mode base |
| Light Background | #F8F6F3 | Light mode base |

## Typography
| Use | Font | Weight |
|-----|------|--------|
| Display/Headings | Plus Jakarta Sans | 700, 800 |
| Body (English) | Inter | 400, 500 |
| Body (Chinese) | Source Han Sans SC / Noto Sans SC | 400, 500 |

## Brand Applications (from design spec)
1. **Business Card** - Dark background with color logo
2. **Mobile App Icon** - Molecular icon on gradient background
3. **Packaging** - Branded box with logo
4. **T-Shirt** - Logo on apparel
